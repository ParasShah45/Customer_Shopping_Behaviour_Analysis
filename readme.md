## Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using an end-to-end Data Analytics workflow. The raw dataset was cleaned and transformed using Python, stored in a MySQL database, analyzed using SQL queries, and finally visualized through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

The objective of this project is to uncover customer purchasing patterns, revenue drivers, product performance, and customer engagement trends to support data-driven business decisions.

## Key Findings
Revenue Insights
- Male customers generated higher total revenue compared to female customers.
- Young Adult customers contributed the highest share of revenue among all age groups.
- Clothing emerged as the highest revenue-generating product category.

Customer Behavior Insights
- Returning customers formed the largest customer segment, indicating strong customer retention.
- Repeat buyers were more likely to have active subscriptions than one-time buyers.
- Subscription customers contributed a significant portion of overall revenue.

Product Insights
- Several products achieved consistently high review ratings, demonstrating strong customer satisfaction.
- Footwear received the highest average rating among product categories.
- Outerwear generated the lowest revenue contribution.

Discount Insights
- Certain products showed a high percentage of purchases made with discounts applied.
- Customers using discounts frequently spent above the overall average purchase amount, suggesting discounts can drive larger transactions.


## Business Recommendations
Customer Retention
- Develop loyalty programs targeting returning customers.
- Reward repeat buyers with exclusive offers and subscription benefits.

Subscription Growth
- Introduce subscription-based discounts and early-access promotions.
- Target non-subscribers with personalized marketing campaigns.

Category Optimization
- Continue investing in high-performing categories such as Clothing and Accessories.
- Investigate reasons behind lower revenue in Outerwear and create targeted promotions.

Discount Strategy
- Apply discounts strategically to products with proven discount-driven sales performance.
- Monitor discount effectiveness to maximize revenue without reducing profitability.

Customer Experience
- Analyze characteristics of highly-rated products and apply those learnings to lower-rated categories.
- Focus on maintaining customer satisfaction to improve retention and repeat purchases.


## DAX Measures
### Average Purchase Amount

Average Purchase Amount =
AVERAGE(customer_behaviour[purchase_amount])

### Average Review Rating

Average Review Rating =
AVERAGE(customer_behaviour[review_rating])

### Number of Customers

Number of Customers =
COUNT(customer_behaviour[customer_id])

### Total Revenue

Total Revenue =
SUM(customer_behaviour[purchase_amount])
